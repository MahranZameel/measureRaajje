Help

Basic use of Measure Raajjé

You will need to select your destination before you add a route. Measure Raajje will show errors if the following steps are not carried out.

  1. Select a destination atoll

    Measure Raajje, by default, selects the departing location as Male. But you will have to select a destination atoll and island before pressing the 'Add Route' button. Therefore, please select a destination atoll first.

  2. Select a destination island

    Once you select a destination atoll, a list of islands belonging to the atoll will be presented to you. You can now pick your destination island from that list.

  3. Add route

    After you have selected your desination, you can press the Add Route button to get your results.

Other Tips

  • Customize your transportation

    If you know the speed, mileage and the cost of fuel, and is different from the defualt values provided, you can change the values by pressing the down arrow in the transportation box and entering them in the relevant fields. Do make sure to change your values before you add the route.

  • Mobile viewing

    When viewing on small mobile devices, turn the device into landscape mode to view the full results table. However, please do keep in mind that some devices might still not be able to support the full view.
