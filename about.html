Measure Raajjé

Measure Raajje is a website designed to provide easy access to distance information between islands of the Maldives. The main purpose of the website is to empower the people who are travelling, especially in speed boats and dhonis, with the knowledge of how much time and money they are spending on a trip.

The website is developed to be used as a tool to plan and bugdet the trip.
